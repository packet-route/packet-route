## What is an AP course

[College Board program, college-level material in high school, ends in a national exam scored 1 to 5, colleges may grant credit.]

## What is AP CSP

[AP Computer Science Principles overview: broad introduction to computing covering programming, data, the internet, and the impact of computing. Less code-heavy than AP CS A.]

## Course structure: the Big Ideas

AP CSP is organized into five Big Ideas:

1. Creative Development
2. Data
3. Algorithms and Programming
4. Computer Systems and Networks
5. Impact of Computing

## The Performance Task

[The Create Performance Task: students design and develop a program, document it, and submit it as part of the AP score. Roughly 30% of the exam score.]
